History & Major Conflicts

1945–1954

First Indochina War

Against French colonial forces; led to Geneva Accords and division.

1955–1975

Vietnam War (Second Indochina War)

North vs. South; ended with reunification in 1976.

1978–1989

Cambodian–Vietnamese War

Intervention to remove Khmer Rouge.

1979

Sino-Vietnamese War

Brief border conflict with China.

1988

Johnson South Reef Skirmish

Naval conflict with China in Spratly Islands.

Present

Maritime Tensions

Territorial claims in the South China Sea (East Sea).

Cultural Context

While a large majority identify as having "no religion" in a formal sense, the vast majority of Vietnamese participate in syncretic folk religions and ancestor worship practices that form the backbone of national cultural identity.
